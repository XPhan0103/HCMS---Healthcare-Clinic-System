Survey & Analysis of HCMS Project Stakeholders (Pediatrics)
Version: v0.1

Power/Interest Grid Analysis

Mapping the involvement of individuals and external users interacting with the lean clinic management system (MVP Scope).

Stakeholder / Role Power Interest Objectives & Expectations BA Management Strategy
Sponsor / Decider
Lead Doctor (Chủ PM) 		High High Requires 100% digital Pediatric EMR tracking without paper. Needs a straightforward billing solution for out-of-pocket costs without complex insurance flows. Manage Closely: Provide them with the core 5-entity database architectural plan. Keep them completely isolated from bloatware and complex features.
Key User
Receptionist (Lễ tân) 		Med High Requires relief from overwhelming phone appointment bookings during peak hours. Needs a system that displays clear Paid/Unpaid billing statuses. Keep Satisfied: Demonstrate how the self-service booking portal intercepts and handles traffic. Validate the checkout UI with them for maximum speed.
External User
Parents (Phụ huynh) 		Low High Expects to securely and effortlessly book pediatric consultations online anytime, tracking the child's medical notes directly. Monitor (Design Focus): The booking portal UX must be completely frictionless on mobile browsers. Must prevent dropped requests or slow loading.
Consultant / IT
BA Leader / PM 		High Med Committed to maintaining the strict project oath - an MVP with strictly 5 tables (Patient, Appointment, Visit, Prescription, Billing) to ensure swift go-live. Keep Informed: Track scope discipline weekly. Ensure absolutely no accidental inclusion of Health Insurance or Advanced Pharmacy logic.

📝 BA Note: Since the clinic operates on a very lean model (1 Receptionist, 1-2 Doctors), any system complexity creates an immediate bottleneck. The UX must focus on minimum click paths (1-click checkout) and zero-maintenance self-service for parents.
